Dowry Prohibition Act

The Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961, makes giving or taking dowry illegal in India. Anyone demanding, giving, or facilitating dowry can face imprisonment up to 5 years and a fine.

Section 3, 4, and 5 of Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961

Domestic Violence Protection

The Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005 provides protection to women from physical, emotional, sexual, verbal, and economic abuse.

Cruelty by Husband

Section 498A of IPC deals with cruelty by husband or relatives. If a woman is subjected to cruelty or harassment for dowry, the accused can face imprisonment up to 3 years and fine.

Dowry Death

Section 304B IPC addresses dowry death. Punishment includes minimum 7 years to life imprisonment in applicable cases.
