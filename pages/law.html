Know Your Legal Rights

Every woman deserves protection, respect, and justice. Learn about laws that safeguard your dignity and rights.

Dowry Violence Protection

The Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961, makes giving or taking dowry a punishable offense. Victims can seek police help and protection under this law.

Domestic Violence Act, 2005

This Act provides protection to women from physical, emotional, verbal, sexual, and economic abuse within the household. It ensures the right to residence and legal support.

IPC Section 498A

Section 498A of the Indian Penal Code safeguards women from cruelty by husband or relatives. Offenders face imprisonment and fines under this section.

Helpline & Legal Aid

Women in distress can reach out to 181 (Women Helpline), 1091 (Police Helpline), or contact free legal aid services under the National Legal Services Authority (NALSA).
